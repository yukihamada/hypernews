## Recording Tips

### Screen Recording
1. **Clean browser**: No bookmarks, history, or personal info visible
2. **Zoom level**: 100% (no browser zoom)
3. **Cursor**: Use large cursor for visibility
4. **Speed**: 1.5x speed for boring parts (typing, loading)
5. **Highlights**: Circle or arrow for important elements
6. **Transitions**: 0.3s crossfade between scenes

### Voiceover
1. **Microphone**: Good quality (Blue Yeti, Rode, or similar)
2. **Environment**: Quiet room, minimal echo
3. **Tone**: Enthusiastic but not cheesy, conversational
4. **Pacing**: Clear enunciation, natural rhythm
5. **Breathing**: Edit out loud breaths
6. **Volume**: Normalize to -14 LUFS for web platforms

### Music
1. **Source**: Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or royalty-free (YouTube Audio Library)
2. **Genre**: Tech, upbeat, energetic (think Apple product launch)
3. **BPM**: 120-140 (matches pacing)
4. **Mix**: Music at -20dB when voiceover plays, -12dB otherwise
5. **Ending**: Fade out over 2 seconds

### Editing
1. **Software**: DaVinci Resolve (free), Final Cut Pro, or Premiere Pro
2. **Framerate**: 30fps or 60fps
3. **Color grading**: Slight contrast boost, vibrant colors
4. **Captions**: Add subtitles for accessibility (auto-generate, then proof)
5. **Export**: H.264, 1080p, 10Mbps bitrate
